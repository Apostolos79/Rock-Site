Sonar Festival, Barcelona

Sónar is the place to be for lovers of electro music...

View details »

Sónar is the place to be for lovers of electro music. Created in 1994, the festival takes place over three days during the day and at night. Thousands of participants from all over Europe enjoy concerts given by some of the biggest names in electro music as well as by up-and-coming stars. A European institution and an internationally recognised event, Sónar festival goes hand in hand with the festive atmosphere that Barcelona does so well.

Street Mode Festival, Thessaloniki, Greece

Street Mode Festival is an annual music, art and sports festival held in Thessaloniki ...

View details »

Street Mode Festival is an annual music, art and sports festival held in Thessaloniki since 2009. It is the largest festival in North Greece, reaching more than 40.000 visitors, in a 4-day long program involving live music gigs and street activities. All forms and modes of urban music and culture have a place in the line-up of Street Mode festival, a program that involves live music gigs, dj sets, shows and contests of graffiti, street art, breaking, street dance, parkour, freerunning, skateboarding, longboarding & more.
